Goldcrest

The original iteration of this model revolved around the small colour change on the top of the bird's head, hence goldcrest. However, I later reworked the details and discovered that there were many further colour changes possible on the top side of the model, allowing it to become more of a general small passerine. These details are however not represented in the digrams yet.

Difficulty: Intermediate

Paper recommendation: 15-20cm dual colour
