About

Conor Nally

I am a first year PhD student at the University of Edinburgh, at the Royal Observatory on BlackFord Hill. I work under Dr Olivia Jones and Prof. Annette Ferguson, we are studying asymptotic giant branch stars with resolve stellar data from James Webb Space Telescope.
