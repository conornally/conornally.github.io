About

Conor Nally

I am a first year PhD student at the University of Edinburgh, in the Royal Observatory on BlackFord Hill. I work under the supervision of Dr. Olivia Jones and Prof. Annette Ferguson. We are studying evolved stellar populations within local group objects and their role in chemical enrichments in the ISM.
